Welcome to Ruins Cafe

Experience the flavors of the Cordilleras at Ruins Cafe. Enjoy our selection of traditional breakfast meals, freshly brewed coffee, refreshing teas, local specialties, and delicious desserts.

🍳 Breakfast Specials

Start your day with customer favorites such as Ruins Longanisa, Tinapang Bangus, Roberto's Arroz Caldo, Mt. Data Tapa Rice, and French Toast. Our breakfast menu combines local flavors with hearty portions.

☕ Coffee Selection

Enjoy freshly prepared coffee beverages including Ruins Espresso, Americano, Cappuccino, Cafe Latte, and other specialty drinks. Perfect for relaxing, studying, or spending time with friends.

🍵 Tea and Refreshments

Discover refreshing drinks such as Moroccan Mint Tea, Ruins Iced Tea, Iced Latte, and Iced Mocha. Our beverage selection offers something for every preference.

🍽️ Local Favorites

Taste authentic local dishes including Pinikpikan and Bagnet Fries. These specialties showcase the rich culinary traditions of the region.

🍫 Desserts and Sweet Treats

Complete your dining experience with Rizal's Tsokolate-e, Champorado, and Iced Chocolate Shake. These desserts are perfect for sharing with family and friends.
